About

Born, raised and educated in Milan, after spending several years working in Italy, I have been traveling around the world, which always had been my dream. I love to meet new people, to see new places, cultures, and to introduce Italian culture.

In Milan, Paris, London, Sydney, Hong Kong, Shanghai…, I captured various key kitchen positions at different restaurants and renowned hotels, such as Mariott, Belvedere, Vanetian, Sofitel Legend Metropole Hanoi (the first Sofitel Legend hotel), Intercontinental.
